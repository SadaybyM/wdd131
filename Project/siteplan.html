Free Veterinary Clinic - Site Plan

Site Name

VetCare Free Clinic - This name reflects the mission of providing free veterinary services in the community.

Site Purpose

This website will serve as an information hub for pet owners, providing details about available veterinary services, pet adoption, and how to support animal welfare initiatives.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find free veterinary services for my pet?
  • How can I adopt a pet from the clinic?
  • What volunteer opportunities are available at the clinic?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #4CAF50 (used for headers and navigation)

Secondary Color: #F4A261 (used for buttons and highlights)

Typography

Headings: Anton SC

Body Text: Roboto
